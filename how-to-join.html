How To Join

Joining WonderWomen (WW) is a great way to find support. If you are an Orthodox Jewish woman under 45 at diagnosis and within three years of your breast cancer diagnosis (Stage 1-4), we invite you to reach out.

To ensure the best experience for everyone, please contact Alizah or Sasha to arrange a brief conversation. We look forward to connecting with you!
