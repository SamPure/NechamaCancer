24/6 Support

Our 24/6 support network provides continuous emotional and practical support for Orthodox Jewish women throughout their breast cancer journey, respecting Shabbat observance.

We understand that cancer doesn't take breaks, and neither should your support system. Our network of volunteers and survivors is available 24 hours a day, 6 days a week, providing:

  • Emotional support and understanding from women who have walked the same path
  • Practical advice for navigating treatment and recovery
  • Help with religious observance during treatment
  • Connection to resources and services
  • Emergency support when needed

Our support respects Orthodox Jewish values and traditions, ensuring that women can maintain their religious observance while receiving the care and support they need.
