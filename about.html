About WonderWomen

Supporting and empowering young Orthodox Jewish women through their breast cancer journey

Our Mission

To provide a supportive network for young Orthodox Jewish women diagnosed with breast cancer, offering emotional support, practical resources, and a community of understanding.

We believe that no woman should face breast cancer alone. Our mission is to create a safe, nurturing environment where young Orthodox Jewish women can find strength, hope, and practical support throughout their journey.

Our Story

Founded by women who understand the unique challenges faced by young Orthodox Jewish women with breast cancer, WonderWomen has grown into a vital support network.

Our organization was born from the recognition that young Orthodox Jewish women face unique challenges when diagnosed with breast cancer. From maintaining religious observance during treatment to navigating family dynamics and community expectations, these women need specialized support that understands their cultural and religious context.

What started as a small group of women supporting each other has grown into a comprehensive community that serves hundreds of women across the country, providing everything from emotional support to practical resources.

Who Is This Group For

Young Orthodox Jewish women who have been diagnosed with breast cancer and are seeking support, understanding, and community during their journey.

Young Orthodox Jewish women (typically under 50)
Recently diagnosed or currently in treatment
Survivors seeking ongoing support
Women seeking culturally-sensitive support
