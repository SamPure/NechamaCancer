What We Do

Comprehensive support services tailored to your needs

24/6 Support

Round-the-clock support through our dedicated community, ensuring you're never alone in your journey.

Always Available

Our support network is available 24 hours a day, 6 days a week (respecting Shabbat). Whether you need someone to talk to at 2 AM or want to share a victory, we're here.

Peer Support

Connect with women who truly understand what you're going through. Our peer support system matches you with women at similar stages in their journey.

Professional Guidance

Access to counselors and healthcare professionals who understand the unique needs of Orthodox Jewish women.

Monthly Speakers

Expert speakers and educational sessions covering medical, emotional, and practical aspects of breast cancer.

Medical Experts

Oncologists, surgeons, and specialists who explain treatments, side effects, and recovery processes.

Emotional Wellness

Psychologists and therapists who address the emotional challenges of breast cancer diagnosis and treatment.

Practical Living

Nutritionists, fitness experts, and lifestyle coaches who help you maintain health and wellness.

Spiritual Support

Rabbis and spiritual leaders who provide guidance on maintaining faith and religious observance during treatment.

Bags for Bravery (B4B)

Care packages filled with essential items and comfort goods for women beginning their treatment journey.

What's Included:

Comfort items for treatment days
Educational materials and resources
Personal care products
Treatment tracking tools
Spiritual and religious items
Comforting treats and surprises

Gatherings

Regular meetups and social events to build connections and foster community among members.

  • Monthly in-person or virtual gatherings
  • Workshops, social events, and holiday celebrations
  • Opportunities to share experiences and support each other

Shabbatons

Special weekend retreats combining spiritual nourishment with community support and relaxation.

  • Shabbat retreats in beautiful, peaceful settings
  • Guest speakers, group activities, and time for reflection
  • Strengthen your faith and build lifelong friendships
