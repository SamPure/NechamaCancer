Shabbatons

Our Shabbaton retreats provide a unique opportunity for spiritual healing, community building, and rest in a supportive environment that honors Orthodox Jewish traditions.

These special weekend retreats combine the beauty of Shabbat observance with the power of community support, offering:

  • Spiritual programming and prayer services
  • Educational sessions and workshops
  • Time for reflection and personal growth
  • Community meals and social activities
  • Support group sessions
  • Beautiful accommodations and kosher dining

Shabbatons are designed to provide a complete break from the stresses of daily life while fostering deep connections with other women who understand the challenges of breast cancer. They offer a chance to recharge spiritually and emotionally in a nurturing environment.
