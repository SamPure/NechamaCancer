Bags for Bravery (B4B)

Our Bags for Bravery program provides thoughtfully curated care packages to women who are newly diagnosed with breast cancer, offering comfort and practical items during a challenging time.

Each B4B bag contains carefully selected items designed to provide comfort, support, and practical assistance during treatment, including:

  • Comfort items for treatment sessions
  • Educational materials and resources
  • Self-care products
  • Religious items and prayer books
  • Practical tools for managing treatment side effects
  • Information about our support network

These bags are delivered with love and care by our community volunteers, often serving as the first point of connection between a newly diagnosed woman and our support network.
