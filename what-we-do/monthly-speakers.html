Monthly Speakers

Our monthly speaker series brings together experts, survivors, and healthcare professionals to provide valuable insights and education for our community.

Each month, we host informative sessions covering topics such as:

  • Medical updates and treatment options
  • Nutrition and wellness during treatment
  • Mental health and emotional well-being
  • Navigating insurance and healthcare systems
  • Survivorship and life after treatment
  • Religious observance during treatment
  • Family dynamics and relationships

These sessions provide an opportunity to learn from experts while connecting with other women in our community. All sessions are designed to be culturally sensitive and respectful of Orthodox Jewish values.
