We are WonderWomen

WonderWomen is a community dedicated to empowering and supporting young Orthodox Jewish women diagnosed with breast cancer.

Learn More Get Support

About WonderWomen

Supporting and empowering young Orthodox Jewish women through their breast cancer journey

Our Mission

To provide a supportive network for young Orthodox Jewish women diagnosed with breast cancer, offering emotional support, practical resources, and a community of understanding.

Our Story

Founded by women who understand the unique challenges faced by young Orthodox Jewish women with breast cancer, WonderWomen has grown into a vital support network.

Who Is This Group For

Young Orthodox Jewish women who have been diagnosed with breast cancer and are seeking support, understanding, and community during their journey.

What We Do

Comprehensive support services tailored to your needs

24/6 Support

Round-the-clock support through our dedicated community, ensuring you're never alone in your journey.

Monthly Speakers

Expert speakers and educational sessions covering medical, emotional, and practical aspects of breast cancer.

Bags for Bravery (B4B)

Care packages filled with essential items and comfort goods for women beginning their treatment journey.

Gatherings

Regular meetups and social events to build connections and foster community among members.

Shabbatons

Special weekend retreats combining spiritual nourishment with community support and relaxation.

Additional Support

Specialized programs to enhance your well-being

Magnificent Me

A specialized program focused on self-care, confidence building, and personal empowerment during and after treatment.
